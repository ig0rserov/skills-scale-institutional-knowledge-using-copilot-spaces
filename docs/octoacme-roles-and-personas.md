# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Quality Lead

### Role Summary
Quality Leads define and drive quality standards across the product. They coordinate testing strategies, establish quality metrics, and ensure that features meet acceptance criteria before release.

### Responsibilities
- Define quality standards and testing strategies
- Coordinate test planning and execution across teams
- Establish and track quality metrics (test coverage, defect rates, performance)
- Review and approve release readiness from a quality perspective
- Mentor team members on testing best practices
- Identify quality risks and recommend mitigation strategies

### Goals
- Maintain high product quality and reliability
- Reduce customer-impacting defects
- Ensure comprehensive test coverage across critical flows
- Enable fast, confident releases

### Typical Communication
- Weekly quality reviews with Project and Product Managers
- Test strategy documentation and test plans
- Quality metrics dashboards and release readiness reports
- Bug triage meetings and retrospectives

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Works closely with **Developers** to define testability requirements and review test coverage
- Collaborates with **Product Managers** to understand acceptance criteria and success metrics
- Partners with **Project Managers** to assess quality risks and release readiness
- Coordinates with **DevOps Engineers** on test automation infrastructure and CI/CD pipeline quality gates

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge the gap between business stakeholders and technical teams. They gather requirements, analyze business processes, and translate stakeholder needs into clear, actionable specifications.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit and document business requirements from stakeholders
- Analyze current processes and identify improvement opportunities
- Create functional specifications, user stories, and acceptance criteria
- Validate that delivered solutions meet business needs
- Facilitate requirements workshops and refinement sessions
- Maintain traceability between requirements and deliverables

### Goals
- Ensure clear, complete requirements that align with business objectives
- Reduce rework caused by unclear or missing requirements
- Improve stakeholder satisfaction through effective communication
- Enable efficient prioritization and planning

### Typical Communication
- Requirements documentation (user stories, process flows, specifications)
- Stakeholder interviews and workshops
- Sprint refinement and backlog grooming sessions
- Requirements traceability matrices

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Partners with **Product Managers** to understand product vision and translate it into detailed requirements
- Works with **Stakeholder Advocates** to gather and validate stakeholder needs
- Collaborates with **Developers** and **Quality Leads** to clarify requirements and define acceptance criteria
- Supports **Project Managers** with scope definition and change management

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers design, implement, and maintain the infrastructure and automation that enables rapid, reliable software delivery. They bridge development and operations to enable continuous integration, deployment, and monitoring.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines and automation
- Manage infrastructure as code (IaC) and cloud resources
- Implement monitoring, logging, and alerting systems
- Ensure security, compliance, and disaster recovery capabilities
- Optimize build times and deployment processes
- Support incident response and troubleshooting

### Goals
- Enable fast, safe, automated deployments
- Maintain high system reliability and availability
- Reduce manual toil through automation
- Ensure infrastructure scalability and cost-efficiency

### Typical Communication
- Runbooks and operational documentation
- Incident reports and postmortems
- Infrastructure design documents
- Deployment and release coordination

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Collaborates with **Developers** to design deployment strategies and troubleshoot issues
- Partners with **Quality Leads** to integrate automated testing into CI/CD pipelines
- Works with **Project Managers** to plan infrastructure changes and assess deployment risks
- Coordinates with **Product Managers** on feature flags, rollout strategies, and monitoring of business metrics

---

## Stakeholder Advocate

### Role Summary
Stakeholder Advocates represent and champion stakeholder interests throughout the project lifecycle. They ensure stakeholder voices are heard, concerns are addressed, and expectations are managed effectively.

### Responsibilities
- Identify and map stakeholders and their interests
- Gather and communicate stakeholder feedback and concerns
- Facilitate stakeholder engagement and alignment
- Manage stakeholder expectations throughout the project
- Escalate critical stakeholder issues to leadership
- Ensure stakeholder sign-off at key milestones

### Goals
- Maintain strong stakeholder relationships and trust
- Ensure stakeholder needs are considered in decision-making
- Reduce surprises and misalignment
- Increase stakeholder satisfaction with project outcomes

### Typical Communication
- Stakeholder briefings and status updates
- Feedback summaries and concern reports
- Stakeholder engagement plans
- Escalation documentation

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Collaborates with **Project Managers** on stakeholder communication plans and status reporting
- Partners with **Product Managers** to align stakeholder needs with product priorities
- Works with **Business Analysts** to validate requirements reflect stakeholder needs
- Coordinates with all team members to gather information for stakeholder updates
